Popular Mobile Legends Betting Markets

Mobile Legends offers a wide variety of betting options. Here are the most popular types of Mobile Legends bets available to Filipino players:

Match Winner

The simplest form of Mobile Legends betting where you pick which team will win the match. Also known as moneyline betting.

Handicap

One team is given a virtual advantage or disadvantage to balance the odds. Popular for matches with clear favorites.

Total Maps

Betting on whether the total number of games played in a series will be over or under a specified number (typically in best-of-3 or best-of-5 series).

First Blood

Betting on which team will get the first kill in a match. A popular market for Mobile Legends due to the importance of early game advantages.

First to X Kills

Betting on which team will be the first to reach a certain number of kills (e.g., first to 10 kills).

First Tower

Betting on which team will destroy the first tower in the match. Towers provide gold and map control, making this a strategic betting market.

First Turtle/Lord

Betting on which team will secure the first Turtle or Lord objective. These objectives provide significant advantages in Mobile Legends.

Total Kills Over/Under

Betting on whether the total number of kills in the match will be over or under a specified number. This market is influenced by team playstyles (aggressive vs. passive).

Match Duration

Betting on how long the match will last, typically in minutes. Mobile Legends matches can range from quick 12-minute stomps to 30+ minute back-and-forth battles.

Mobile Legends Betting Tips for Filipino Players

Improve your chances of winning with these Mobile Legends betting strategies:

  • Study team playstyles - Some teams prefer aggressive early game strategies, while others excel in late game teamfights.
  • Follow the meta - Stay updated on patch changes and how they affect hero viability and team strategies.
  • Research head-to-head records - Some teams consistently perform well against specific opponents regardless of form.
  • Consider player specialties - Many pro players have signature heroes they excel with. Watch for these in the draft.
  • Analyze the draft phase - The hero draft often determines the outcome before the match even begins.
  • Watch for roster changes - Team performance can change dramatically with new players or substitutions.
  • Consider tournament context - Teams may perform differently in regular season matches versus playoffs or international tournaments.
  • Look for value bets - Sometimes the odds don't accurately reflect the true probabilities, creating betting opportunities.
