What is Online Sabong?

Sabong, or cockfighting, is a traditional Filipino sport with centuries of history in the Philippines. Types of Sabong Bets

Online Sabong offers several betting options for Filipino players:

Meron/Wala

The most common bet type. "Meron" refers to the rooster on the right side, while "Wala" refers to the rooster on the left side.

Draw

Betting that the match will end in a draw. This has higher odds but is less common in Sabong.

Handicap

Some platforms offer handicap betting where one rooster is given a virtual advantage to balance the odds.

First Blood

Betting on which rooster will draw first blood in the match.

Match Duration

Betting on how long the cockfight will last, typically in minutes or rounds.

Parlay

Combining multiple Sabong bets into one wager for higher potential payouts. All selections must win for the parlay to pay out.

Online Sabong Betting Tips

Improve your chances of winning with these Sabong betting strategies:

  • Study the roosters - Pay attention to the roosters' physical attributes, including weight, height, feather condition, and overall appearance.
  • Check the gaff - The type and length of the blade (gaff) attached to the rooster's leg can significantly impact the outcome.
  • Research bloodlines - Some bloodlines are known for specific fighting styles and strengths. Knowledge of these can give you an edge.
  • Watch previous fights - Many online platforms maintain archives of past matches. Study these to understand fighting patterns.
  • Manage your bankroll - Set a budget for your Sabong betting and stick to it. Never chase losses.
  • Start with small bets - Begin with smaller stakes until you gain experience and confidence in your betting strategy.
  • Avoid emotional betting - Make decisions based on analysis rather than emotions or hunches.

Legal Status of Online Sabong in the Philippines

The legal status of online Sabong in the Philippines has evolved over time. Traditional cockfighting has been legal and regulated in the country for centuries, with local government units issuing permits for cockpit operations. In recent years, online Sabong (e-Sabong) gained popularity, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.

In 2021, the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation (PAGCOR) began regulating online Sabong operations, issuing licenses to qualified operators. However, in May 2022, the government temporarily suspended e-Sabong operations nationwide due to social concerns.

Currently, the regulatory landscape for online Sabong continues to evolve. Many Filipino players access international Sabong betting platforms that operate outside Philippine jurisdiction. When choosing an online Sabong site, it's advisable to select platforms with proper licensing from recognized gaming authorities.
