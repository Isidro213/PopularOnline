Popular Esports Betting Markets

Esports offers a wide variety of betting options. Here are the most popular types of esports bets available to Filipino players:

Match Winner

The simplest form of esports betting where you pick which team will win the match. Also known as moneyline or 1X2 betting.

Handicap

One team is given a virtual advantage or disadvantage to balance the odds. Popular for matches with clear favorites.

Total Maps/Games

Betting on whether the total number of maps or games played in a series will be over or under a specified number.

First Blood

Betting on which team will get the first kill in a match. Popular in MOBA games like Mobile Legends and DOTA 2.

First to X Kills

Betting on which team will be the first to reach a certain number of kills (e.g., first to 10 kills).

Tournament Winner

A long-term bet on which team will win an entire tournament. These bets typically offer higher odds.

Map Winner

Betting on the winner of a specific map in a multi-map series, rather than the overall match winner.

Prop Bets

Special bets on specific events within a match, such as which team will destroy the first tower or take the first objective.

Live Betting

Placing bets during a match as the action unfolds, with odds that change in real-time based on the current situation.

Esports Betting Tips for Filipino Players

Improve your chances of winning with these esports betting strategies:

  • Specialize in specific games - Focus on games you understand well rather than betting on multiple esports titles.
  • Research team form and rosters - Stay updated on team performance, roster changes, and player statistics.
  • Follow the meta - Game updates and meta changes can significantly impact team performance, especially in MOBAs.
  • Consider team matchups - Some teams perform better against certain opponents due to playstyle differences.
  • Watch for value bets - Look for situations where the odds don't accurately reflect the true probabilities.
  • Manage your bankroll - Set a budget for your esports betting and never bet more than you can afford to lose.
  • Use live betting strategically - In-play betting can offer value when you spot momentum shifts or draft advantages.
  • Follow professional analysis - Listen to expert opinions and analysis from professional esports commentators.

Mobile Legends Betting Guide

As the most popular mobile esport in the Philippines, Mobile Legends deserves special attention for Filipino bettors:

Key Factors to Consider

  • Draft phase - Hero picks and bans can determine the outcome before the match even begins.
  • Team composition - Look for balanced teams with clear strategies (e.g., early game aggression vs. late game scaling).
  • Player specialties - Some players excel with specific heroes or roles.
  • Objective control - Teams that prioritize objectives like turtles and lords often have higher win rates.
