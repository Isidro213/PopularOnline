Cashback bonuses are perfect for:

Weekly Cashback

Understanding Cashback Terms

Cashback offers come with specific terms and conditions. Here are the key things to look for:

  • Cashback Percentage: The percentage of your net losses that will be returned (typically 5-25%)
  • Calculation Period: How often cashback is calculated and credited (daily, weekly, or monthly)
  • Eligible Games: Which games contribute to cashback calculations (some offers are specific to slots or live casino)
  • Minimum Loss Requirement: The minimum amount you need to lose to qualify for cashback
  • Maximum Cashback: The maximum amount of cashback you can receive per period
  • Wagering Requirements: How many times you need to bet your cashback before withdrawing (lower is better)
