Important Terms to Check

No deposit bonuses always come with terms and conditions. Here are the key things to look for:

  • Wagering Requirements: How many times you need to bet the bonus amount before withdrawing (typically higher for no deposit bonuses)
  • Maximum Cashout: The maximum amount you can withdraw from your no deposit bonus winnings
  • Game Restrictions: Which games you can play with your bonus (often limited to specific slots)
  • Time Limits: How long you have to use the bonus and meet wagering requirements (usually shorter than deposit bonuses)
  • Bet Size Limits: Maximum bet allowed when playing with bonus funds (often around ₱50-₱100)
